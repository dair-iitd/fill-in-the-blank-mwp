Q: There are 15 trees in the grove. Grove workers will plant _____ trees in the grove today. After they are done, how many trees would be there?
A: 21 
Rephrased: There are 15 trees in the grove. Grove workers will plant some trees in the grove today. After they are done, there would be 21 trees. Find the number of trees planted.
Answer: There are 15 trees originally, Then there were 21 trees after some more were planted. So there must have been 21 - 15 = 6 trees. The answer is 6.

Q: If there are 3 cars in the parking lot and _____ more cars arrive, how many cars are in the parking lot?
A: 5
Rephrased: If there are 3 cars in the parking lot and some more cars arrive, there are 5 cars in the parking lot. Find the number of cars that arrived.
Answer: There are originally 3 cars. There are 5 cars after some more cars arrive. 5 - 3 = 2, so 2 cars arrived. The answer is 2.

Q: Leah had 32 chocolates and her sister had _____. If they ate 35, how many pieces do they have left in total?
A: 39
Rephrased: Leah had 32 chocolates and her sister had some more. If they ate 35, they had 39 pieces left in total. Find the number of chocolates her sister had initially.
Answer: Originally, Leah had 32 chocolates. Together, they had a total of 35 + 39 = 74 chocolates. Therefore, Leah's sister originally had 74 - 32 = 42 chocolates. The answer is 42.

Q: Jason had 20 lollipops. He gave Denny some lollipops. Now Jason has _____ lollipops. How many lollipops did Jason give to Denny?

Q: Shawn has five toys. For Christmas, he got _____ toys each from his mom and dad. How many toys does he have now?
A: 9
Rephrased: Shawn has five toys. For Christmas, he got an equal number of toys from both his mom and dad. He has 9 toys now. Find the number of toys he received from each parent.
Answer: Shawn started with 5 toys. If he has 9 toys now, he recieved 9 - 5 = 4 toys in total. Since he received an equal number from both, he would have got 4 / 2 = 2 toys from each parent. The answer is 2.

Q: There were nine computers in the server room. _____ more computers were installed each day, from monday to thursday. How many computers are now in the server room?
A: 29
Rephrased: There were nine computers in the server room. Some more computers were installed each day, from monday to thursday. There are 29 computers now in the server room. Find the number of computers installed daily.
Answer: There were originally 9 computers. Overall, 29 - 9 = 20 more computers were added. Since they were added over 4 days, 20 / 4 = 5 computers were installed daily. The answer is 5.
