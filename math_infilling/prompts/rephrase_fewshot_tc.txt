Q: There are 15 trees in the grove. Grove workers will plant _____ trees in the grove today. After they are done, how many trees would be there?
A: 21
Solution: Rephrased: There are 15 trees in the grove. Grove workers will plant x trees in the grove today. After they are done, there would be 21 trees. Find the value of x.
Answer: There are 15 trees originally, Then there were 21 trees after some more were planted. So there must have been x = 21 - 15 = 6 trees. The answer is 6.

Q: If there are 3 cars in the parking lot and _____ more cars arrive, how many cars are in the parking lot?
A: 5
Solution: Rephrased: If there are 3 cars in the parking lot and x more cars arrive, there are 5 cars in the parking lot. Find the value of x.
Answer: There are originally 3 cars. x more cars arrive. 3 + x = 5, so x = 5 - 3 = 2. The answer is 2.

Q: Leah had 32 chocolates and her sister had _____. If they ate 35, how many pieces do they have left in total?
A: 39
Solution: Rephrased: Leah had 32 chocolates and her sister had x. If they ate 35, they had 39 pieces left in total. Find the value of x.
Answer: Originally, Leah had 32 chocolates. Her sister had x. So in total they had 32 + x. After eating 35, they had 32 + x - 35 = x - 3. Since they had 39 chocolates left, x - 3 = 39, so x = 39 + 3 = 42. The answer is 42.

Q: Jason had 20 lollipops. He gave Denny some lollipops. Now Jason has _____ lollipops. How many lollipops did Jason give to Denny?
A: 8
Solution: Rephrased: Jason had 20 lollipops. He gave Denny 8 lollipops. Now Jason has x lollipops. Find the value of x.
Answer: Jason started with 20 lollipops. Then he gave Denny 8. So Jason has x = 20 - 8 = 12 lollipops. The answer is 12.

Q: Shawn has five toys. For Christmas, he got _____ toys each from his mom and dad. How many toys does he have now?
A: 9
Solution: Rephrased: Shawn has five toys. For Christmas, he got x toys each from his mom and dad. He has 9 toys now. Find the value of x.
Answer: Shawn started with 5 toys. If he got x toys each from his mom and dad, then that is 2 * x more toys. 5 + 2 * x = 9, so 2 * x = ( 9 - 5 ) = 4. x = 4 / 2 = 2. The answer is 2.

Q: There were nine computers in the server room. _____ more computers were installed each day, from monday to thursday. How many computers are now in the server room?
A: 29
Solution: Rephrased: There were nine computers in the server room. x more computers were installed each day, from monday to thursday. There are 29 computers now in the server room. Find the value of x.
Answer: There were originally 9 computers. For each of 4 days, x more computers were added. Since the total number of computers is 29, 9 + 4 * x = 29. So 4 * x = 29 - 9 = 20, and x = 20 / 4 = 5. The answer is 5.

Q: Michael had 58 golf balls. On tuesday, he lost _____ golf balls. On wednesday, he lost 2 more. How many golf balls did he have at the end of wednesday?
A: 33
Solution: Rephrased: Michael had 58 golf balls. On tuesday, he lost x golf balls. On wednesday, he lost 2 more. He had 33 golf balls at the end of wednesday. Find the value of x.
Answer: Michael started with 58 golf balls. After losing x on tuesday, he had 58 - x. = 35. After losing 2 more, he had 58 - x - 2 = 56 - x golf balls. At the end of wednesday, he had 56 - x = 33 golf balls. So x = 56 - 33 = 23. The answer is 23.
